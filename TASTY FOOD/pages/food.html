Souvlaki is a popular Greek dish featuring skewered and grilled pieces of marinated meat, often lamb, chicken, or pork. Served with pita bread, tzatziki sauce, and a variety of vegetables, it's a flavorful and satisfying meal that's beloved both in Greece and around the world.

FROM THE GRILL

T-BONE STEAK $5.95

A T-bone steak is a prime cut of beef featuring a T-shaped bone with meat on both sides. One side typically contains tenderloin, known for its tenderness, while the other side has strip steak, prized for its rich flavor. It's a favorite among steak lovers for its combination of tenderness and bold taste, perfect for grilling or pan-searing to perfection.

RUMP STEAK $5.95

Rump steak, also known as sirloin steak in some regions, is a flavorful and versatile cut of beef. It's taken from the hindquarters of the cow and is known for its robust beefy flavor and slight chewiness. While it's not as tender as some other cuts, it's still quite juicy and delicious when cooked properly. Rump steak is great for grilling, pan-searing, or broiling, and it pairs well with a variety of sauces and sides.

SIRLOIN STEAK $5.95

Sirloin steak is a popular and versatile cut of beef taken from the back of the cow, near the loin area. It's known for its balance of tenderness and flavor, making it a favorite choice for grilling, broiling, or pan-searing. Sirloin steaks can vary in tenderness depending on the specific area of the loin they are cut from, but they generally offer a juicy and beefy taste that pairs well with a variety of seasonings and accompaniments.

PASTA

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian dish consisting of spaghetti pasta topped with a rich meat sauce. The sauce typically includes ground beef, tomatoes, onions, garlic, carrots, celery, and a variety of herbs and spices. It's a hearty and comforting meal that's loved by many for its delicious flavors and satisfying texture.

CHICKEN STROGANOFF $5.95

Chicken Stroganoff is a variation of the classic beef Stroganoff dish, originating from Russia. It features tender pieces of chicken cooked in a creamy sauce made with sour cream, onions, mushrooms, and sometimes a splash of white wine or broth. The dish is typically seasoned with herbs and spices like paprika and served over egg noodles or rice. It's a comforting and flavorful dish that's perfect for a cozy dinner at home.

FETTUCINE ALFREDO $5.95

Fettuccine Alfredo is a classic Italian pasta dish consisting of long, flat noodles (fettuccine) tossed in a creamy sauce made with butter, heavy cream, and grated Parmesan cheese. Sometimes garlic is added for extra flavor, and parsley or black pepper may be used as a garnish. The result is a rich and indulgent pasta dish that's creamy, savory, and utterly satisfying.
